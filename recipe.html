1. WHITE SAUCE PASTA


INGREDIENTS:

BOILING: 2 cups Penne Pasta (200 gms), Salt to taste, 1/2 tsp oil

VEGETABLES: 2 tsp olive/butter, 1/2 onion chopped, 1 small carrot,
1/2 capsicum chopped, handful of corns, salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper powder

SAUCE: 4 tbsp butter, 4 tbsp maida, 2 cups milk, Salt,
1/2 tsp black pepper powder, 1/2 tsp red chilly flakes,
1/2 tsp mixed herb seasoning, 4-5 tbsp grated cheese
